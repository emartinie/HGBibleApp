The Premise

Every serious study of the New Testament eventually arrives at a Hebrew source, a Jewish parallel, a textual echo that only makes sense in its original context. This workspace was built for that moment — when you need to hold two traditions in your hands at once.

The Method

The tools here do not interpret for you. They place witnesses alongside each other. The Masoretic text beside the Septuagint. The NT quotation beside its Hebrew source. The rabbinic reading beside the apostolic one. The Word speaks first.
