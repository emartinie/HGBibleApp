The Premise

Every serious study of the New Testament eventually arrives at a Hebrew source, a Jewish parallel, a textual echo. This workspace was built for that moment — when you need to hold two traditions at once.

The Method

The tools here do not interpret for you. They place witnesses alongside each other. The Masoretic text beside the Septuagint. The NT quotation beside its Hebrew source. The Word speaks first.
