What I would not pull into HomeGroups

To keep the experience focused, I'd avoid making the app a mirror of Sefaria. I would leave out or defer:

Full Siddur and prayer books
Most Kabbalistic works
Deep Halakhic libraries
Thousands of niche commentaries
Advanced academic collections
User account features
User-generated Source Sheets (except curated examples)

The sweet spot, in my opinion, is using Sefaria as a Jewish knowledge engine rather than as another reading website. HomeGroups becomes the guided experience that surfaces exactly the right Jewish context—commentary, Midrash, cross-references, topics, people, and calendar connections—at the moment the reader needs them, instead of requiring them to sift through thousands of linked texts. That plays to the strengths of both platforms.
