Welcome to HomeGroups

About HomeGroups

HomeGroups is a community-centered movement dedicated to helping people study, test, and live the Scriptures together. It welcomes serious questions, careful investigation, and differing conclusions while encouraging people to pursue truth with humility, trust, and responsibility.

The HG Bible App is the primary study environment created to support that mission. It brings together the weekly studies—our shared discipleship experience—with readings from the Torah, Prophets, Writings, and New Testament, as well as language tools, historical context, maps, calendars, prayer, community resources, and longer-form investigations.

The HomeGroups Research Institute supports the movement by investigating difficult biblical, historical, and related questions. Its investigations distinguish between direct evidence, reasonable inference, tradition, interpretation, and speculation while clearly identifying what remains uncertain.

HomeGroups was built because biblical study is often scattered across disconnected books, websites, traditions, and institutions. Our goal is not to tell people what they must think, but to help people study carefully, test claims honestly, live what they learn, and encourage one another as lifelong students of Scripture.
