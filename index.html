This Week's Portion

Parashat Kedoshim

Leviticus 19:1–20:27 • Called to holiness. Deep covenant living.

📖 Torah Text ▼
The Portion
Kedoshim, "the holy ones," centers Israel's calling: to embody holiness in everyday life—justice, honesty, compassion. The laws are not abstract rules but the texture of covenant relationship with the Divine and each other.
✨ Key Verses ▼
Leviticus 19:2
"Be holy, for I, the Lord your God, am holy."
Leviticus 19:18
"Love your neighbor as yourself. I am the Lord."
