Parashat Kedoshim

Leviticus 19:1–20:27 • A call to holiness and sacred living.

📖 Weekly Scripture ▼
Torah Portion
Parashat Kedoshim teaches the foundations of ethical living and covenant relationship. It encompasses the laws of holiness, the treatment of the poor and elderly, honest weights and measures, and the commandment to love your neighbor as yourself.
✨ Key Verses ▼
Leviticus 19:2 — "Speak to the entire assembly of Israel and say to them: 'Be holy because I, the Lord your God, am holy.'"
Leviticus 19:18 — "Love your neighbor as yourself. I am the Lord."
