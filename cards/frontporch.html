🔬 Natural Processes Perspective

This view explains the universe and life through natural processes such as physics, chemistry, astronomy, and biological evolution. It emphasizes observable and testable mechanisms.

  • The universe developed over time
  • Complexity can emerge from simpler systems
  • Scientific models focus on how processes work

⚖️ A Shared Question

Both perspectives are trying to answer the same deep question: Why is there something rather than nothing?

Science often explains how things work. Faith traditions often explore why they exist. Many people find themselves living in the tension between both.
