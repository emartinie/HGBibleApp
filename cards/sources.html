Witness of the People of God

Scripture has been recognized, confessed, prayed, translated, and preserved within the life of God’s people across generations.

  • Canon — These writings were received as Scripture through use and trust.
  • Creeds — Core truths were confessed publicly as a summary of shared faith.
  • Prayer & Liturgy — Scripture shaped daily prayer, worship, and communal life.
  • Translation — God’s Word crossed languages so people might hear and understand.
  • Preservation — The text endured because it mattered enough to be copied and protected.

This witness reflects how Scripture was lived before it was analyzed.
