Witness of the People of God

Scripture has been recognized, confessed, prayed, translated, and preserved within the life of God’s people across generations. This witness reflects lived faithfulness rather than scholarly analysis.

  • Canon — These writings were received as Scripture through use and trust, not imposed by force.
  • Creeds — Core truths were confessed publicly, often under persecution, as a summary of shared faith.
  • Prayer & Liturgy — Scripture shaped daily prayer, worship, and communal life.
  • Translation — God’s Word crossed languages so that people might hear and understand.
  • Preservation — The text endured because it mattered enough to be copied, protected, and suffered for.

This witness reflects how Scripture was lived before it was analyzed.
