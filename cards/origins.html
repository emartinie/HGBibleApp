Origins

Where do we come from? Why is there something instead of nothing? This is not a debate stage. It is a table. You are welcome here whether you come with conviction, curiosity, questions, or uncertainty.

Explore. Reflect. Go deeper only when you want to.

🕊️ Creation / Design Perspective

This view holds that the universe, life, and humanity were created intentionally by a Creator. In Biblical tradition, the Creator is personal, purposeful, and involved.

  • Order and beauty suggest intention
  • Life appears meaningful, not accidental
  • Human consciousness and morality point beyond matter alone

🔬 Natural Processes Perspective

This view explains the universe and life through natural processes such as physics, chemistry, astronomy, and biological evolution. It emphasizes observable and testable mechanisms.

  • The universe developed over time
  • Complexity can emerge from simpler systems
  • Scientific models focus on how processes work

⚖️ A Shared Question

Both perspectives are trying to answer the same deep question: Why is there something rather than nothing?

Science often explains how things work. Faith traditions often explore why they exist. Many people find themselves living in the tension between both.
