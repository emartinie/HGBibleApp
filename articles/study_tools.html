The Bible is, in essence, the Christian handbook for life. It contains God’s Word to man, his instructions for living. Therefore, reading and studying the Bible is one of the most important essentials in spiritual development. Yet many new believers don’t know where to start on this seemingly daunting task. These tools, tips and techniques are meant to simplify the process of Bible study and challenge Christians at any stage in their walk to make the most of their Bible study time.

Study to show thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth. 2Timothy 2:15

This website is intended to give you all the help you need putting information together. We have Study Guides for every chapter in the New Testament here: https://homegroups.org/equipping/bible-studies/

Every year, I choose to take my family through the complete Bible now that my children are becoming old enough to participate.

Pr 22:6 ¶ Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it.
