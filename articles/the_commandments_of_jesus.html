In the “old testament” there are at least 613 commandments. And when Jesus (Yeshuah) walked the Earth He gave some insight into the heart and spirit of those commandments. Did He add to commandments of God, or did He expound on God’s commandments? Did He do away with God’s commandments and replace them with His own? Other pages and posts deal with these questions, so here we will simply record the “commands” that Jesus gave to men on Earth. This is not a full list and it will grow in time. Enjoy!

2. “Come unto Me” Matt. 11:28
