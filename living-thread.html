Practice Step

The Word Becomes A Walk

  • Pause before one conversation today and silently pray: "Let me be a meeting place, not a wall."
  • Read Psalm 27 aloud once, slowly, without multitasking.
  • Write one sentence: Where did heaven and earth feel closest today?

The journey closes the loop: reading becomes attention, attention becomes obedience, obedience becomes witness.
